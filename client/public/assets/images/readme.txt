Jaceface's Pillars of Eternity Portrait Pack
==================***=======================

Custom portraits for Pillars of Eternity and Deadfire

=======Installation=======

Unzip the downloaded file, then copy and paste the two folders (female and male) into:

\Steam\SteamApps\common\Pillars of Eternity\PillarsOfEternity_Data\data\art\gui\portraits\player

for steam users and:

\Pillars of Eternity\PillarsOfEternity_Data\data\art\gui\portraits\player

for GoG retail buyers.
